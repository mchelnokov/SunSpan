# SunSpan Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-06_

SunSpan is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your data.

## Summary

SunSpan does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

## Location

If you grant the app permission, SunSpan reads your device's location **once per launch** to show daylight data for where you are. Your location is used **only on your device** to compute solar geometry (sunrise, sunset, twilight times) and to display a pin on the in-app map. It is never transmitted to us, never stored in any cloud service, and never shared with third parties.

You can deny or revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → SunSpan. The app will then fall back to a default location, which you can change manually using the in-app map.

When you select a location on the in-app map, SunSpan uses Apple's on-device CoreLocation reverse-geocoder to look up the place name and time zone. This lookup is handled by Apple's system frameworks and is subject to [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Collection

SunSpan collects **no** data of any kind. Specifically, SunSpan does **not** collect:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, advertising identifiers)
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Location data (beyond the on-device use described above)
- Contacts, photos, calendars, or any other personal content
- Payment or financial information

## Network Activity

SunSpan does **not** make any network requests of its own. All solar calculations are performed locally on your device. The only external service the app touches is Apple's CoreLocation reverse-geocoder, which is invoked through iOS system frameworks when you choose a location on the map.

## Children

SunSpan does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published in this repository, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
